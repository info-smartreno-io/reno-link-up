## Typical Timeline After Materials Arrive

<Callout type="info" title="Material Lead Times Are Critical">
These timelines assume materials are on-site. In 2025, custom cabinets and specialty items can take 8–12 weeks to arrive. Order early!
</Callout>

**Typical after-materials:**  
- **Kitchens:** 6–10 weeks  
- **Bathrooms:** 3–6 weeks  
- **Basements:** 6–10 weeks  
- **Additions:** 12–24+ weeks

## What Adds Time

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Timeline Extenders">
**Adds time:** HOA/condo approvals (Hudson County), complex plumbing moves, custom millwork, structural changes, and permit delays in busy season.
</Callout>

**Factors that extend timelines:**
- HOA approvals (2–4 weeks in Hudson County)
- Zoning variances (6–12 weeks if needed)
- Custom cabinetry (8–12 week lead time)
- Structural work requiring engineering
- Asbestos/lead abatement
- Winter weather delays
- Inspector availability

## What Saves Time

**Saves time:** early ordering, clean permit packet, decisive selections.

<Callout type="success" title="Speed Up Your Project">
Making final material selections before construction starts, having permits ready, and ordering long-lead items early can save 4–8 weeks on total timeline.
</Callout>

**Timeline accelerators:**
- Pre-ordering materials (especially cabinets)
- Having complete permit applications ready
- Making all selections before demo
- Working with experienced local contractors
- Scheduling during off-peak season
- Maintaining clear communication

## Phase-by-Phase Breakdown

### Kitchen Remodel (8 weeks typical)
- Week 1: Demo and rough-ins
- Week 2–3: Electrical, plumbing, inspections
- Week 4–5: Drywall, paint, flooring
- Week 6–7: Cabinet installation
- Week 8: Counters, backsplash, appliances

### Bathroom Renovation (4 weeks typical)
- Week 1: Demo and rough-in
- Week 2: Tile work
- Week 3: Fixtures and finishes
- Week 4: Final details and cleanup

### Basement Finishing (8 weeks typical)
- Week 1–2: Framing and mechanicals
- Week 3–4: Rough inspections and insulation
- Week 5–6: Drywall and paint
- Week 7–8: Flooring and trim
