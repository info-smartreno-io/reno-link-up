## Pre-Work Requirements

<Callout type="warning" title="Start with HOA Approval">
Always submit your HOA **architectural packet** before scheduling contractors. Most Hudson County buildings require 2–4 weeks for review and approval.
</Callout>

**Pre-work checklist:**  
- HOA **architectural packet** (scope, drawings, insurance, license)
- **Work hours** + **noise compliance** verification
- Elevator and loading dock bookings
- **Protection plan** (hallways, lobby, unit)
- Certificate of insurance from contractor

## Municipal Permits

<Callout type="info" title="Permit Requirements">
Beyond HOA approval, you need municipal permits for plumbing/electrical work. Some buildings require **sprinkler/FD sign-off** for major renovations.
</Callout>

**Permits:** plumbing/electrical; some buildings require **sprinkler/FD sign-off**.

## Logistics Planning

**Logistics:** tight material staging, daily debris removal, room-by-room sequencing.

<Callout type="success" title="Condo-Specific Experience Matters">
Work with contractors who specialize in **Hudson County condo projects**. They understand building requirements, have relationships with property managers, and know how to minimize disruption.
</Callout>

## Common Building Restrictions

- Work hours: Typically 9 AM–5 PM weekdays only
- No weekend work in most buildings
- Elevator reservations required (book weeks in advance)
- Daily cleanup and debris removal mandatory
- Noise restrictions on drilling/hammering
