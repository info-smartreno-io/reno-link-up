## Hudson County Condo Pricing

<Callout type="info" title="Condo Premium">
Hudson County condo renovations typically cost 10–20% more than single-family homes due to logistics, building requirements, and work restrictions.
</Callout>

**Kitchens (condo, mid-range):** $28k–$55k+  
**Bathrooms (condo):** $18k–$35k+

## Unique Cost Factors

Add line items for: **elevator/dock booking**, protection, quiet-hours, daily disposal, and routing long materials.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hidden Condo Costs">
Don't forget to budget for building-specific fees, elevator reservations, hallway protection, and daily waste removal requirements.
</Callout>

### Logistics Premiums

**Typical add-ons:**
- Elevator reservations: $50–$200/day
- Building access fees: $500–$2,000
- Hallway/lobby protection: $300–$800
- Daily waste removal: $75–$150/day
- Off-hours premium: 15–25% labor increase

## Kitchen Cost Breakdown

**Typical mid-range condo kitchen:**
- Cabinets: $10k–$22k
- Countertops: $3k–$8k
- Appliances: $4k–$12k (paneled add $2k–$4k)
- Backsplash: $1.5k–$4k
- Electrical: $2k–$5k
- Plumbing: $1.5k–$4k
- Flooring: $2k–$5k
- Labor premium for condo: $3k–$7k

<Callout type="success" title="Where to Splurge">
In **Hoboken and Jersey City**, prioritize durable, easy-clean finishes. Quartz counters and quality hardware see heavy daily use and affect resale value.
</Callout>

## Bathroom Cost Breakdown

**Typical condo bathroom:**
- Tile (floor + walls): $3k–$8k
- Vanity and countertop: $1.5k–$4k
- Toilet: $300–$800
- Shower/tub: $1k–$5k
- Plumbing fixtures: $800–$2.5k
- Electrical/ventilation: $1k–$2.5k
- Labor and logistics: $5k–$10k

## Timeline Considerations

<Callout type="warning" title="Building Restrictions Add Time">
Work-hour restrictions, elevator scheduling, and inspection coordination can add 1–3 weeks to standard timelines in Hudson County condos.
</Callout>

**Typical timelines:**
- Kitchen: 8–12 weeks (vs. 6–10 in houses)
- Bathroom: 4–7 weeks (vs. 3–6 in houses)
- Add 2–4 weeks for HOA approval before work starts

## Money-Saving Tips

- Order materials to arrive just-in-time (storage is expensive)
- Consolidate deliveries to minimize elevator fees
- Choose in-stock materials over custom orders
- Schedule during off-peak season (winter) when possible
- Work with contractors experienced in Hudson County condos
