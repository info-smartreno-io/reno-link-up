## Complete Permit Checklist

<Callout type="info" title="Why This Matters">
Proper permits protect your investment, ensure code compliance, and prevent issues when selling your home. Most North Jersey towns actively enforce permit requirements.
</Callout>

**Checklist includes:**  
- Scope summary (fixtures, circuits, venting)
- Drawings/photos as required
- **Plumbing + electrical** applications
- Rough + final inspection booking
- Smoke/CO compliance (as required)

## Pre-Submission Requirements

<Callout type="success" title="Prepare These Documents">
Having complete documentation upfront speeds approval and reduces back-and-forth with building departments.
</Callout>

**Required documents:**
- Site plan showing work location
- Floor plans with fixture locations
- Electrical layout and load calculations
- Plumbing diagram with venting path
- Product specifications for major items
- Contractor license and insurance certificates

## Inspection Process

**Rough inspections check:**
- Framing and blocking
- Electrical rough-in (wire, boxes, circuits)
- Plumbing rough-in (supply, drain, venting)
- HVAC ductwork if applicable

**Final inspections verify:**
- All fixtures properly installed
- GFCI/AFCI protection in place
- Proper venting and clearances
- Smoke/CO detectors as required
- Code-compliant finishes

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Cover Work Early">
Never cover walls, ceilings, or plumbing before rough inspection. This is the most common reason for failed inspections and costly rework.
</Callout>

## Timeline Guidelines

- **Application processing:** 1–4 weeks (varies by town)
- **Inspection scheduling:** Book 2–3 days in advance
- **Re-inspection if needed:** 3–5 days
- **Certificate of completion:** 1–2 weeks after final approval
