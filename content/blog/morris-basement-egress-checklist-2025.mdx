## Code Requirements

<Callout type="error" title="Egress is Non-Negotiable">
**Sleeping rooms** need **egress** (window or door) with compliant **clear width/height**. This is a critical safety requirement enforced by all Morris County municipalities.
</Callout>

**Code highlights:**  
- **Moisture control** (sump/french drain where needed)
- **Ceiling height** minimum requirements (typically 7 feet)
- **Mechanical clearances** (furnace, panel access)
- **Fireblocking** & insulation R-values
- **GFCI protection** for all outlets

## Major Cost Drivers

<Callout type="info" title="Budget Planning">
Key expenses include: framing complexity, bath rough-ins, egress cutting, waterproofing systems, and sound control between floors. A professional assessment prevents costly surprises.
</Callout>

- **Framing & drywall:** $8–$15/sq ft
- **Egress window installation:** $3k–$8k
- **Bathroom addition:** $12k–$25k
- **Waterproofing system:** $2k–$6k
- **Flooring (LVP/carpet):** $4–$10/sq ft
