## How SmartReno Works

<Callout type="success" title="Streamlined & Transparent">
Our process eliminates the hassle of coordinating multiple estimates while ensuring competitive pricing and quality contractors for your North Jersey renovation.
</Callout>

### Step 1: On-Site Estimator Visit

**On-site estimator** captures measurements + photos during a single 60-minute visit to your home.

**What happens:**
- Detailed measurements of work areas
- Photos documenting existing conditions
- Discussion of your goals and timeline
- Code and permit requirement review
- Material preferences and budget discussion

### Step 2: Detailed Scope & Estimate

**Scope + estimate** delivered for review within 3–5 business days.

<Callout type="info" title="Transparent Pricing">
You receive a line-item breakdown showing exactly what is included, excluded, and optional. No hidden surprises.
</Callout>

**Your estimate includes:**
- Detailed scope of work
- Material specifications
- Labor breakdown
- Timeline estimates
- Permit requirements
- Allowances clearly marked

### Step 3: Three Competitive Bids

**3 vetted bids** from our network of licensed, insured contractors.

**All bidders are:**
- Licensed NJ Home Improvement Contractors
- Fully insured (general liability + workers comp)
- Reference-verified with recent local projects
- Experienced in your specific project type
- Familiar with your town's requirements

### Step 4: Select Your Contractor

**Select contractor** with schedule and warranty clarity.

<Callout type="success" title="Compare Apples to Apples">
Since all contractors bid on the same scope, you can confidently compare pricing, timeline, and approach without confusion.
</Callout>

**Compare:**
- Price and payment schedule
- Proposed timeline
- Warranty terms
- Communication approach
- References and past work

### Step 5: Milestone-Based Payments

**Milestone payments** ensure quality and accountability throughout the project.

**Typical payment structure:**
- 10% deposit at contract signing
- 25% at rough-in completion
- 35% at substantial completion
- 25% at final walkthrough
- 5% held for 30-day warranty period

<Callout type="warning" title="Protected Payments">
Your payments are tied to verified milestones, not arbitrary dates. This protects both you and the contractor.
</Callout>
