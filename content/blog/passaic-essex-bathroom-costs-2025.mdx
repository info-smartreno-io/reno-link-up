**Ballpark (hall bath, mid-range):** $18k–$32k  
**Primary bath:** $28k–$55k+ (steam, custom vanity, large format tile)

## Key Cost Drivers in Passaic & Essex

<Callout type="info" title="What Affects Your Budget">
- **Tile scope** (large format, shower pan, niches)
- **Plumbing moves** (stack location in older Montclair/Maplewood homes)
- **Electrical** (dedicated circuits, fan/sconce upgrades)
- **Lead times** (stone, custom glass, specialty fixtures)
</Callout>

## Permit Requirements

**Permits:** Most towns (Clifton, Montclair, Newark, West Orange) require **plumbing + electrical**. Plan for rough and final inspections.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip for Smooth Permitting">
Submit a **clean scope** with fixture locations, venting path, and GFCI plan to minimize back-and-forth with the building department.
</Callout>

## Timeline Expectations

- **Design & selections:** 1–2 weeks
- **Permit review:** 1–3 weeks (varies by town)
- **Construction:** 3–6 weeks after materials arrive
- **Final inspection:** Plan 3–5 days lead time

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Delays">
Custom tile and specialty fixtures can take 6–10 weeks to arrive. Order early to keep your project on schedule.
</Callout>
