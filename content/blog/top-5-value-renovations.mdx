Some upgrades shine brighter than others around **Bergen, Passaic, Morris, Essex, and Hudson County**:

1. **Kitchen Refresh or Redesign** — Layout + storage + durable finishes. (Popular in **Ridgewood, Montclair, Hoboken**.)
2. **Bathroom Upgrades** — Tile, vanity, lighting, ventilation. (High demand in **Clifton, West Orange, Jersey City**.)
3. **Finished Basements** — New living/office/play space. (Code‑aware egress in **Morris County**.)
4. **Thoughtful Additions** — Bedrooms, family rooms, or bump‑outs. (Frequent across **Wyckoff, Livingston, Maplewood**.)
5. **Exterior Improvements** — Roofing, siding, windows for curb appeal + efficiency (notably in **Paramus, Wayne, Bayonne**).
